Research Interests

My research focuses on AI for Science & Bioinformatics, targeting the analysis and interpretation of biomedical data (molecules).
I am interested in theoretical foundations of machine learning: Causal Inference, Invariant Representations, Information Bottleneck, Multi-view Learning, XAI, and Fairness.
I am also interested in GNNs, Graph Transformers, and Large Language Models for knowledge discovery.
