About

My research focuses on AI for Bioinformatics, developing strategies to analyze and interpret biomedical data such as molecular structures. I am particularly interested in using Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Large Language Models (LLMs) for knowledge discovery.

Research Interests

  • Foundation Models: Pre-training & Adaptation, Self-Supervised Learning
  • Graph Learning: Graph Structure Learning, Molecular Learning, Knowledge Graphs
  • AI for Bioinformatics: GNNs and Graph Transformers
  • Theoretical Foundations: Information Theory, Causal Inference, Multi-view Learning
  • Explainable AI and Fairness Learning
