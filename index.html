Hi, I’m Van Thuy Hoang, a PhD Student of the Catholic University of Korea. I’m a member of the Network Science Lab led by Professor O-Joun Lee .

If you would like to contact me or collaborate, please feel free to drop me an email.

Research Interests

My research focuses on AI for Bioinformatics, developing strategies to analyze and interpret biomedical data such as molecules. I am particularly interested in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), Graph Transformers, and Large Language Models (LLMs) for knowledge discovery.
  • Foundation Models: Pre-training & Adaptation strategies, Self-Supervised Learning
  • Graph Learning: Graph Structure Learning, Molecular Learning, Knowledge Graphs
  • AI for Bioinformatics: Graph Neural Networks, Graph Transformers
  • Theoretical Foundations: Information Theory, Causal Inference, Multi-view Learning, Explainable AI, Fairness Learning, and Pattern Recognition.
