Van Thuy Hoang

Ph.D. Student in Artificial Intelligence
The Catholic University of Korea

My research focuses on AI for Bioinformatics, developing strategies to analyze and interpret biomedical data such as molecular structures. I am particularly interested in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), Graph Transformers, and Large Language Models (LLMs) for knowledge discovery.

Research Interests

  • Foundation Models: Pre-training & Adaptation, Self-Supervised Learning
  • Graph Learning: Graph Structure Learning, Molecular Learning, Knowledge Graphs
  • AI for Bioinformatics: Graph Neural Networks, Graph Transformers
  • Theoretical Foundations: Information Theory, Causal Inference, Multi-view Learning
  • Explainable AI and Fairness Learning
